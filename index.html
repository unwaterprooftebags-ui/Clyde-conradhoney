Our Story

Clyde & Conrad Honey Co. was founded on a simple belief: exceptional honey begins with healthy bees and a deep respect for the land they live on. Our hives are carefully tended in the countryside surrounding Adderbury, where bees forage freely among wildflowers, clover fields, and hedgerows.

We work in harmony with the seasons, producing small batches of organic honey using traditional, sustainable beekeeping methods. Our bees are never exposed to chemicals or artificial feeding, and we harvest only what the hive can naturally spare.

Every jar is handled with care and bottled at peak freshness to preserve flavour, texture, and natural goodness. What you enjoy is honey in its purest form — local, honest, and full of character.

Why Natural

We believe that honey is at its very best when it is left exactly as nature creates it. All-natural honey offers deeper flavour, a richer aroma, and a more complex character that reflects the flowers and landscapes from which it comes.

Unlike mass-produced honey, our honey contains no preservatives, no additives, and no unnecessary processing. We do not heat, dilute, or alter our honey — ensuring that its natural texture, taste, and integrity remain intact.

Choosing natural honey also means choosing a healthier and more sustainable product. By keeping our honey raw and unprocessed, we retain the qualities that make it a nourishing, wholesome food, just as it has been enjoyed for generations.
